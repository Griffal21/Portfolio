About Me

Upon graduating from the University of Scranton with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics I took a gap year in my studies where I primarially tutored High School and Middle School Math, Science, Social Studies, and Standarized Exam Prep. During this time I also earned a trade school degree as an automotive technician. My studies resumed the following year with the goal of becoming a secondary mathematics teacher. During such time I also took a job at Six Flags Great Adventure as a Unit Supervisor, Staffing Supervisor, and Labor Analyst. I excelled at Rider until I reaching student teaching and I realized teaching was not for me. I then enrolled in Rutgers University's coding bootcamp to become fluent in multiple coding languages. It was here I leaned HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Ajax, React, and MySQL just to name a few.

For this I have listed my most common jobs I would perfrom, which I would job depended on the situation, day, and availiable personnel.

  • Found solutions for various scheduling and personality conflicts for our employees
  • Recorded employee data in company databases
As a Staffing Supervisor
